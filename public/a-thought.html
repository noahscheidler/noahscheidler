Noah Scheidler

Poetry Collection, Vol. 1

Une Pensée

A life is the story of your thoughts, so write it.

Self-published by the author

I was born happy

Thanks to mum

So we will do better and better still

Creating is the most beautiful thing

That makes failure just as much so

To fail, then do it better because we must succeed

Never doubt again and be sure of oneself

To feed on one's suffering

To know where we come from and where we want to go

To repair our mistakes or move forward with them

To stop at no failure

To believe and begin again, begin again...

I met a girl who marked me.

I know down to the smallest detail what I love and what I cannot stand.

But above all, I know where I am going.

Because where I had never seen it before, I found a meaning to my life.

And I am no longer afraid of anything concerning myself,

if not the world that surrounds us.

But we have nothing to do with it, we live in a false and manipulative system.

A globally magnificent place overrun with productivity and wealth,

but what does it put above all of that:

exploitation. crime of every kind. racism. injustice.

Factors driven by money and human horror.

Simply, there are actors and there are spectators.

All working in the service of their interests or beliefs.

Multiple and diverse, that is the world.

To be unique is to be yourself.

What would it feel like to be someone else?

I asked myself WHY a great many times;

why am I me,

why am I here and what does this life have to teach us.

In the end I do not really know and I do not care

because the only person who can tell me that

EVERYTHING IS POSSIBLE: is me.

When you do not have a path already laid out, or you do not want one,

life is a rollercoaster that we build and then simulate ourselves.

We must test ourselves, seek answers, push our limits.

Believe in destiny and your actions will take you there,

like the day you will go to paradise. If you believe in it.

Your conscience assimilates it. That is what spirituality is.

Thank you mum.

But wait, I am telling you all of this out of order...

Every person has the capacity to build the edifice of their own conscience,

to choose who they want to be.

But for that you must already know who you want to be,

and above all who you are.

It takes asking yourself an enormous amount of questions,

fill your head up, thinking too much is good as long as we do it for ourselves.

We rarely find answers there but we find a direction

and that is what creates our path toward the authenticity of a life.

An immersive experience in the leading role of your own film.

To be what we love, in order to love what we are.

Press on our strong points to anchor them deeper within us

and pull on the weaker ones to elevate them.

The body holds an energy and a rhythm

that are served by your ability to accept a situation, to live.

The barriers of our conscience, our emotional state

and our capacities are limited by us and us alone.

Because we are the architects of our lives,

from the foundations to the finishing touches.

Certainly the starting point is different for everyone,

but if you start first you must hold your place and deserve it.

While if you start twentieth you must give twenty times more effort

than the first and that is an important thing to know.

Everyone has their role to play, in the way they choose.

In any case, now I know what I want in my life.

I never managed, in my adolescence, to project myself into my future

because I was not sure of myself.

And asking yourself the right questions

is eliminating and illuminating the fog inside your head.

I have been able to meet friends who inspired me,

I would love in turn to be able to inspire them.

Like every person I also make mistakes,

which I overcome through the will to change, repair, evolve and succeed.

To repair you must change and to succeed you must also evolve.

Hold your foundation and strengthen what is vulnerable.

I grew up with my brothers who taught me to be sociable,

spontaneous, natural and competitive.

A little sister in whom I have always placed my love and my care.

Because of that, being the person I am,

I will overcome anything with a smile.

Then I had a second little sister

who put my whole life back into context.

Birth is extraordinary because it transmitted to me far more than everything I have told.

And with one single first look: I saw myself again, in her place.

Then I looked at my own path,

it is not the most beautiful, it is not the most perfect but it is mine.

I am only at the beginning of the preface

and I am the only one who can develop what comes next.

A life is the story of your thoughts, so write it.

Shape your ideas.

Even if we do not always have the choice and cannot always do what we want,

we can fight for who we are and what we love.

For those who love us and those we love.

To say I loved living, I also loved destroying myself

and I rejected my feelings because we always attach ourselves to contradictions

but when I put what is essential back into context,

the foundations of life's meaning, the idea of creation,

the journey of our first days up to the edifice of our conscience.

I understood why I was here: to create and to seek myself without end.

So I needed a foundation, and there is one thing my mother gave me

that I remembered and that no one will ever be able to take from me,

my greatest weapon: my smile and my positivity

that I put in service of my beliefs and my vision.

When I looked at my little sister, I examined that smile which told me

« be happy, that is all there is to it. »

Louna, you too were born happy.

Thanks to mum.

A thought.

Noah Scheidler
