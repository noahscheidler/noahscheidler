Noah Scheidler

Poetry Collection, Vol. 3

Note à Moi Même

So I love my happiness as much as my unhappiness,
given that one offers the other.

Self-published by the author

What we are certain of knowing in reality frightens us far more than what we are not in a position to know. And it is for this reason that it is more natural to move toward the unknown. Reason points the way and the heart does not follow.

Then sadness enters the scene and presents us with a dilemma.

And in what way? Be sad or be happy, but love.

Love in a way you will never tire of, with a sadness that will never be enough.

The kind that puts itself at the service of our productivity, committing the good of the bad. In the firm and tender selection of our limited feelings.

So to heal myself I escape into painting and I free myself through writing. I recreate my story through these unique tools, taken on to console my adventure.

She who repeats like the first hours that founded her, face to face without seeing them pass; a feeling of timelessness.

Where time has stopped, at the evidence of an end captured by my eyes for eternity. I speak of a loved emotion, the only one for which my unhappiness grants a share of efficacy. One does not use shortcuts to evoke the science of being, I explain my calculation through the formula of words.

A problematised interaction in which I offer my answer from the moment of my statement: there is no room for sadness if it is not accompanied.

I know its importance through the omnipresence of its harassment in my thoughts. I write this time about sadness, that reversible subject from one person to the next, an intimate theme that suits the deepest emotions. What represents awkwardness and embarrassment, the will or the refusal, and after all, a fantasy. Sadness is beautiful.

I love what is beautiful but I was afraid of this dream.

Alas my eyes, more than anything, sleep in order to return there because she is my event, my cycle and my moment and I represent her feeling, her verdict and her sentence. So on one side she draws me simply to develop and evolve.

Not to convince or conquer, that is the role of time and it does one thing perfectly, it answers our questions and our problems.

And there is no negative aspect to the evocation of my pattern. Nor is there the presence of a prayer. No desire to please, nor to play.

And even less the hope of going back.

I therefore have the obligation to surrender to everything she offers me after having consumed me. The patience, the restraint, the will, the passion and the commitment; among other things and plainly, love consummated.

It is an endless feeling, on which I will write to the very end and without rest. A diplomacy of emotions, which I resolve to reconcile, through my way of expressing myself. To use my emotions to create, and to bring their benefits to light.

I give everything to my grief without asking anything in return, for it is the only thing that allows me to overcome it. I do not feel the need for a certainty of happiness, as mentioned, I cannot control events. Only my emotions, and as an individual, they guide me.

The simple fact of having ideas, that is my attachment, that is my direction.

It is to my sadness that I speak, through this mediocre text.

I have used it to show my feelings my best sides.

I know that my life is governed by its representation.

I make sure it refuses me, so that I can take it in my arms, surrender it and let it see to what extent it does not deserve me.

In my redemption where I am my own guilty party, for I am my own protagonist.

I can only above all attach myself to my undeniable and authentic happiness to answer the questions of my absurd and illogical life.

In the processing of each sensation, I draw their advantage.

Through the analysis of my feelings, optimism is the preeminence of my being.

There is the answer, I reverse the meaning of my most painful emotion with the aim of making it a prerogative.

Here, whatever my states, I erect the benefits of each of my outbursts; to find their predisposition in the assembly of my emotions.

When I love it is irreversible, it is my weapon to beat my melancholy.

If I have taken much, I will always give more, in an indefinite way for the love of a whole life.

It is an emotion that will always refuse me because I accept it in its entirety, as part of myself. It helps me to think, therefore allows me to influence something I cannot control: my events.

So I live for it, for as a child it was already my enemy, but I now set myself ablaze by taking it into my heart on the same terms as happiness; for what it inspires in me. A paradoxical writing that gives them the same consequences: a fallout.

So I love my happiness as much as my unhappiness, given that one offers the other.

I am happy whatever happens, to reverse sadness and transgress wellbeing.

Noah Scheidler
